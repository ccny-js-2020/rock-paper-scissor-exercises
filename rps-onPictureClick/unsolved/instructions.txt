Rocks Paper Scissors on Picture Click
1. You vs the Computer (use objects)
2. Have game start with a button (Start Game Button)
   - When the browser loads
      - The game content will be hidden
      - The Start button will be shown
   - When the Start Game button is clicked
      - Hide thet start game button
      - For the first 3 seconds, show a loading spinner
      - Hide the spinner after 3 seconds
      - The game content will show up after 3 seconds
3. When the picture of the Rock, the Paper, or the Scissor is clicked
   compare your choice against the computers random choice,
   and pick a winner of the round
4. Record the results of the round on the page
  - Your guess
  - Computer guess
  - Winner of round
  - Your total wins
  - Your winning percentage
  - Ties
  - Your last 10 guesses

Notes:
1. The computer guess will be random: 'rock', 'paper', or 'scissor'
2. Scissor beats Paper
   Rock beats Scissor
   Paper beats Rock
3. You will use the data attribute in this exercise
